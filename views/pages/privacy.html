HOW WE USE COOKIES
A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be placed on your computer's hard drive. When you allow a cookie to be placed on your computer, the file is added and it helps to analyze web traffic or lets the user know when you have visited a particular site in the past. It also allows web applications to respond to you as a unique user. Cookies allow a website to remember your preferences and information about you.

Drawingboardevents.com.sg uses traffic log cookies to help us analyse data about our website's traffic, as well as to identify the pages which are being used by our Customers and Vendors. We also use cookies to fit to our users' needs and preferences. We also use cookies for statistical purposes; and the data is removed from our system as soon as we achieve this purpose.

Cookies also help us to improve our website and provide you with a better user experience. A cookie, in no way, gives us access to the files on your computer, or such other information about you, apart from that which you choose to provide us with.

While most web browsers accept cookies automatically, you can still modify your web browser setting to decline the same. Nevertheless, we encourage the use of cookies for a better user website experience.
